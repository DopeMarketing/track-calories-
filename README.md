# Track calories 

## Overview
A mobile application that allows users to easily log and track their daily calorie consumption through food entries and visual progress monitoring. The app focuses on simplicity and ease of use for maintaining awareness of caloric intake.

## Problem
People struggle to accurately track their calorie consumption throughout the day due to complex interfaces, time-consuming food logging processes, and lack of simple progress visualization

## Target User
Health-conscious individuals who want to monitor their calorie intake for weight management, fitness goals, or general wellness tracking

## Features
- Manual food entry with calorie input
- Daily calorie goal setting
- Basic progress tracking and history
- Simple food database lookup
- Daily/weekly summary views
